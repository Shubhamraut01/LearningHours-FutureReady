About Us

DoleOut is a website for a social cause. Our goal is to provide a platform for the organizations who works for socillly backward, unaware and needy section of the society. There are such charity organiztin who really work hard for the needy people but can't collect enough as people are unaware about them. Our website is a platform for them. They can join us and we will try our best to help them.

We are trying to help such institutions to get more and more help from the people who are willing to do any kind of help weather by giving food, cloths some amount or by helping them directly by participating in the events organised by such organizations.
